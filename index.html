Privacy Policy

The House of Saysy — GarmentERP WhatsApp Bot

Effective date: 8 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 8 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The House of Saysy ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our WhatsApp-based production management assistant ("the Service"), including via the WhatsApp Business Platform and our associated web dashboard.

1. Who this applies to

The Service is used internally by our garment production staff, workers, and administrators to track garment lots through our production pipeline via WhatsApp messages. It is not intended for use by the general public or by individuals outside our authorized workforce and business contacts.

2. Information we collect

CategoryExamples
Contact information WhatsApp phone number, name
Message content Messages you send to the Service, including production updates, quantities, and status replies
Production data Lot numbers, client names, garment stage progress, piece counts, rates, and related job/billing records
Usage data Conversation state and interaction history needed to process multi-step workflows (e.g. creating a new lot)

3. How we use this information

  • To route and process production tasks (e.g. assigning, receiving, and approving garment lot stages)
  • To generate job bills and payment records based on completed work
  • To send you relevant notifications (e.g. new lot assignments, approvals, daily summaries)
  • To detect the intent of your messages and generate natural-language replies, including in English, Hindi, and Gujarati
  • To maintain an audit trail of production and approval activity for accountability purposes

4. How messages are processed

Messages you send are received via the WhatsApp Business Platform (operated by Meta) and processed by our backend to determine intent and generate a reply. Message content may be sent to a third-party AI language model provider (Mistral AI) solely to interpret your message and draft a response. This content is not used by us or our sub-processors to train public AI models beyond what is contractually agreed with that provider for service delivery.

5. Third parties we share data with

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not share your data for third-party advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Data retention

We retain production, billing, and audit records for as long as reasonably necessary for business, accounting, and operational purposes. Conversation state data is retained only as needed to support ongoing interactions and may be periodically cleared once a workflow is complete.

7. Data security

We apply reasonable technical safeguards to protect your information, including access-controlled database policies (Row Level Security), encrypted secret storage for API credentials, and restricted administrative access to production systems. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legitimate business record-keeping requirements
  • Withdraw from using the Service by notifying your administrator

9. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for use by adult workforce members and business contacts only. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
